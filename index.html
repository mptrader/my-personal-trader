Plans sized to your capital

Every tier runs the same core engine. What changes is position sizing and risk ceiling, matched to your account size.

Lot sizes and risk parameters are indicative starting points, automatically adjusted by the bot's position-sizing engine as your account balance changes. All trading involves risk of loss.

From signup to live trading in six steps

The bot is a standalone desktop app that connects to your already-running MT5 terminal — it never modifies MT5 itself. Your funds stay with your broker — we never take custody.
